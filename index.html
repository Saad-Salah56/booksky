Rich dad poor Dad

Robert T.Kiyosaki

"Rich Dad Poor Dad" by Robert Kiyosaki contrasts the differing financial philosophies of two father figures: one who adopts a traditional route of saving and employment (poor dad), and another who emphasizes investing and entrepreneurship (rich dad). The book advocates for financial education and asset building for wealth creation.

The Psychology of Money

Morgan Housel

"The Psychology of Money" by Morgan Housel delves into how individual behaviors and psychological factors impact financial decisions. The book emphasizes that financial success often relies more on behavior and mindset than on technical knowledge.
